The material

Optipus PV embeds organic photovoltaics directly into the surfaces around you.

01

Flexible & lightweight

Ultra-thin polymer film under 350 μm and 300 g/m² — laminates, bends, conforms to any shape.

02

Freedom of design

Any color, any texture, any pattern. Energy becomes part of the surface, not an afterthought.

03

Process-compatible

Integrates with the plastics, composites and technical textiles you already manufacture.

04

Direct & diffuse light

Harvests indoors and outdoors. From 2 klux of office light to 120 klux of full sun.
